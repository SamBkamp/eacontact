Who are we?

Since our foundation in 2015, Exotic Aquaculture has been the world leading supplier and breeder of live jellyfish and jellyfish aquariums. We've worked with over 50 public aquariums in over 20 countries. Aside from our work with public aquariums, we have also worked closely with many Universities and research institutions, also supplying jellyfish and jellyfish culturing systems.

We also run our own 2,500sqft facility here in Hong Kong, where we culture thousands (that is not hyperbole) jellyfish at a time. Aside from growing out jellyfish, we also use this facility to do research on these creatures. Everything from testing the effect of various elements on jellyfish, to food and diet research and everything in between.

The future of STEM and Biology

Many of us here at EA have been through academia in STEM fields and personally know that education in this area, especially for secondary schoolers, still leaves a little to be desired. We want to help spread experiences, knowledge and access to passionate students who are keen to get their hands on real-life biologicial research. Whether we help design and build an aquarium system, host research camps or give talks, we'd like to do it all. Our education team led by someone with over 4 years experience teaching primary and secondary schoolers in STEM fields are well equipped to help realize your ideas for biology education.
